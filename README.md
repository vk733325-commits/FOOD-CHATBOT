FOOD-CHATBOT
A Food Chatbot is an AI-based virtual assistant that helps users browse food menus, place orders, get food recommendations, and receive customer support through a chat interface. It interacts with users in natural language, making the food ordering process faster and more convenient.
The chatbot can display available food items, provide details such as price and ingredients, suggest dishes based on user preferences, and calculate the total bill. It can also track orders, answer frequently asked questions, and collect customer feedback.

Food chatbots are commonly used by restaurants, cafes, food delivery services, and online food ordering platforms to improve customer experience and provide 24/7 assistance.

Key Functions:-
Display food menu
Search food items
Recommend dishes
Place food orders
Calculate bills
Track order status
Answer customer queries
Collect ratings and feedback
Benefits:-
Available 24/7
Faster order processing.
Improved customer satisfaction.
Reduced workload for staff.
Personalized food recommendations.
Chatbot frameworks such as NLTK, ChatterBot, or Dialogflow
Objective.
The main objective of a Food Chatbot is to provide an easy, interactive, and efficient way for customers to order food and obtain information without requiring direct human assistance.
